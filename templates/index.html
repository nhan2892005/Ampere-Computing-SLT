Project description:

A Distributor has a small system, it buys products from suppliers and delivers to small stalls. Currently it needs to build a simple web to manage data for its system.

For the detail, system has many facilities, each facility imports products from many suppliers. Each supplier provides several categories of product. Everyday, each facility has new events for the stock in and stock out then records them to the warehouse and consumption table.
